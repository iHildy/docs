## Start Application

To get approved to raise funds on GreekPoints, you must fill out this form and then wait for approval. This is to avoid fraudulent activity on our platform.

Press the "Start Application" button.

## Business Information

<Steps>
  <Step title="Business Type">
    > "**Tax exempt organization**"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ownership Type">
    > "**Private**"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Legal Business Name">
    Input your legal business name, don't include your "DBA"

    > "Phi Delta Theta Fraternity"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Doing Business As">
    Most Greek organizations have a DBA (Doing Business As), it is likely the name of your chapter.&#x20;

    > "Texas Upsilon" or "Epsilon Theta"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 5">
    Write 2-3 sentences about your organization and the types of philanthropic events you'll host on GreekPoints. This will be read by Finix and not GreekPoints, try to not include slang.

    > Texas Upsilon is a greek life fraternity that hosts a Pie a Phi event every semester where attendees can donate to Live Like Lou for the reward of pieing our organization's members.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 6">
    Input the date of your **chapter** incorporation, not your national's incorporation. This could be your date of installment on your chapter's charter.

    > December 16th, 2016
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 7">
    You need your EIN for opening any bank accounts and paying taxes. Your treasurer officer will likely know it. If they can't find it please reach out to GreekPoints for help.

    > 12-3456789
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tax-Exemption Status Proof">
    COME BACK TO THIS. Your treasuerer officer likely has this.

    > School Tax-Exempt Form
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 8">
    If your chapter has it's own website please input it here. If not, use your instagram link. Please remove the "https://" so that there is only 1.

    > https://www.instagram.com/utdphidelt/
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 9">
    Input your phone number, even though it says not to.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Business Address">
    Input the legal address of your chapter, this is also something that your treasurer officer would know. It's likely your chapter house address or FSL/IFC office mailbox.
